My Profile

Born on Friday 25th, Janauary, 1991 by Mr and Mrs Seguya in Busia district, my ancestral origin is Bukulula village in Kalungu district of Greater Masaka in Uganda. Started my early childhood education from Customs Road Primary School in Busia district before going to Vincentalex Boarding Primary School from 2000-2003 and attained all my secondary education from Hilton High School Mukono from 2004-2009 where I performed exceedingly well and joined Makerere University for a Bachelors degree in Business Computing from 2010-2013 and graduated in January 2014.Then I pursued many Certicates at Greenbridge school of Open Technologies.

Politically, I am staunch member of the Social Democratic Party (SDP) of Uganda which was founded by His Worship Al-Hajj Nasser Ntege Sebagala and Hon. Micheal Mabikke of Makindye East Constituency in Kampala Capital City because the ideas, brilliance and the way those from the Uganda Young Democrats (UYD) articulate issues, their leadership skills and policy of grooming and mentoring new leaders and youth in order to solve the leadership crisis in our society inspired me the most to join them.
